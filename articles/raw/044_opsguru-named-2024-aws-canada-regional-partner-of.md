# OpsGuru Named 2024 AWS Canada Regional Partner of the Year

## Introduction

• Announcement

We are proud to announce that OpsGuru, an AWS Premier Tier Services Partner, has been named the 2024 AWS Canada Regional Partner Award winner. This prestigious recognition, unveiled at AWS re:Invent, celebrates partners who drive innovation, collaboration, and business transformation using AWS technologies.

At OpsGuru, we are known for our innovative approach to cloud consulting. We empower businesses with services such as Clear Path Forward, Generative AI Ideator, and Customer Reliability Engineering (CRE). These offerings reflect our dedication to delivering measurable results and accelerating our customers’ cloud success.

“This award underscores our team’s relentless commitment to excellence and innovation,” I stated. “We are honored to be recognized for our transformative impact and look forward to continuing to lead in cloud transformation.”

Our proven expertise and tailored strategies position us as a trusted partner for organizations navigating the complexities of digital transformation.
